Ways to get involved

Join a volunteer team

(There's plenty of work to do.)

Did you enjoy your festival community service and decide you're ready to step up? Reach out to the team lead! Are you good at copy editing? Consider joining our Policy Committee! Know of some good spots to hang flyers or run a booth? Give being an Area Representative a shot! Do you resonate with a particular Sacred Space at Spirit Haven Ranch? Reach out to that Sacred Space Coordinator to see if they need an assistant! Want to step up and contribute more to maintaining and nurturing Spirit Haven Ranch? Reach out to the Ranch Team - we need your help! Not sure where you'd like to contribute your time, but have a niche skill? Reach out to the Board - they'll know where you can best help!
