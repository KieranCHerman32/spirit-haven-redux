Festival Community Service

Festivals are huge events that require a lot of work to put on.

As an entirely volunteer-run organization, Spirit Haven needs your help to make sure Festivals run as smoothly as possible.

All adults who purchase a Full Event registration are required to do (at least) two hours of community service at Festival.

During the registration process, you'll need to choose two Community Service options.

You may not get your first choice community service option or general time slot, but we do try very hard to accommodate everyone.

You'll make a few new friends no matter what you're up to!
